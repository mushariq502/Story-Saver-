About Instant Story Saver

Simple. Fast. Private. We help you save the moments that matter — in one tap.

What is Instant Story Saver
Version 1.0

Instant Story Saver is a lightweight, privacy-first app that allows users to save stories and status updates from Instagram, Facebook, and WhatsApp. It was built to be reliable, fast, and easy to use — giving you control over your personal collection of moments.

Key Features
One-tap downloads
Save images and videos directly to your device in high quality.
Multi-platform
Support for Instagram, Facebook, and WhatsApp stories (public or with permission).
Privacy-first design
No personal data is collected without consent; everything stays on your device unless you choose otherwise.
Premium experience
Go ad-free, enable advanced integrations and priority support with subscription.
Organize & revisit
Built-in gallery and recent downloads make it easy to revisit saved moments.
Why we built this

In many apps, stories disappear after a short time — often before we get a chance to save what matters. We built Instant Story Saver to give users a reliable, respectful way to preserve those moments. Our focus is on speed, clarity, and giving control back to you.

Security & Privacy

Your trust is important. By design:

  • No collection of personal content without explicit consent.
  • Optional logins (OAuth) handled by the platform — we do not store your credentials.
  • Saved media stays on your device; you control sharing and deletion.
How to use — quick guide

1. Paste a story link or use the in-app paste button.
2. Tap Download — the file saves to your device immediately.
3. For account-only features (Instagram/Facebook), enable Login after subscribing to Premium.

Commitment & Vision
